## 2. Get DOT <a href="#id-51cc" id="id-51cc"></a>

a) To get Polkadot native DOT you can visit one of these [major CEXes it is listed on](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/polkadot-new/#Markets). Options include Binance, kraken, Coinbase and more.

b) Alternatively, using the Stellaswap cross-chain swap feature you can trade a variety of EVM native assets for Moonbeam native xcDOT [here](https://app.stellaswap.com/exchange/cross-chain-swap).

## 3. Get GLMR <a href="#id-5914" id="id-5914"></a>

a) GLMR serves as gas for transactions on Moonbeam. To obtain GLMR, you can use any of the [CEXes it is available on](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/moonbeam/).

b) Alternatively, you can obtain GLMR by [visiting the Gas Swap option](https://app.stellaswap.com/bridge/gas-swap) on Stellaswap and swap a variety of EVM assets or native Polkadot DOT into GLMR.

## 4. Bridge Assets to Moonbeam <a href="#id-35de" id="id-35de"></a>

Bridge assets that are tradeable on Stellaswap to the Moonbeam blockchain. Users can bridge their assets via XCM or through Wormhole, a bridge that facilitates the transfer of Ethereum and other EVM assets between different blockchains.

Conveniently use the XCM transfer tool to exchange PEN and DOT for Moonbeam native xcPEN and xcDOT [via the Stellaswap UI](https://app.stellaswap.com/bridge/xctransfer). Use Stellaswap’s [swap feature](https://app.stellaswap.com/exchange/swap) to exchange xcPEN for xcDOT and vice versa for portfolio balancing.

For this transfer you will connect both your polkadot wallet (Subwallet, Talisman etc) and your EVM wallet (Metamask etc).

## 5. Provide Liquidity in xcPEN/xcDOT <a href="#id-7804" id="id-7804"></a>

Once you have sufficient DOT and PEN find “Pulsar” in the dropdown menu. Choose the pair “PEN/DOT” and click “Deposit”. Or simply [click here](https://app.stellaswap.com/pulsar/add/0xffffffff1fcacbd218edc0eba20fc2308c778080/0xffffffff2257622f345e1acde0d4f46d7d1d77d0). Experienced liquidity providers have the option to select ‘Expert’ range and edit the desired price range to optimize your fees. Newer liquidity providers can consider choosing a wider range like ‘Full Range’ to lower your risk. Enter the amount of PEN and DOT you want to add as liquidity and confirm the transaction.

## 6. Farm rewards <a href="#id-5d79" id="id-5d79"></a>

Early liquidity providers will be rewarded with PEN and STELLA tokens for a liquidity mining period of 3 months. Stellaswap will issue an NFT to represent your LP position. Click on your position and click ‘Farm Now’ then ‘Deposit’. Note, this step is crucial to activate your position for farming rewards. You will automatically receive trading fee rewards without completing this step.
